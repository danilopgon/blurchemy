# Presets

Presets are planned as named collections of image-processing settings and gradient stops. They should provide strong creative starting points, not final one-click filters.

## Planned Starter Presets

### Dream

Soft blur, lifted exposure, warm highlights, and muted violet shadows for dissolved, hazy compositions.

### Dune

Sand, umber, and sun-burnt highlights with moderate grain and restrained contrast.

### Blade Runner

Dense contrast, electric magenta/orange color tension, and heavier grain for nocturnal cinematic work.

### Noir

Near-monochrome palette, high contrast, and strong grain for stark editorial imagery.

### Hotel Sur

Muted plaster, oxidized green, deep brown, and a controlled coral accent. The accent color is `#f55033` and should be used as a highlight or focal interruption, not as a broad wash.

## Rules

- Presets must remain browser-local data.
- Presets should include descriptive metadata for UI browsing.
- Randomization should use pleasing bounded ranges and keep values export-safe.
